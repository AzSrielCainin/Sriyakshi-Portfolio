Product Description
An app which tracks user’s carbon footprint and informs them how to reduce it

Problem statement:

Users do not always understand or know the full extent of how their energy consumption affects their carbon footprint over a period of time.

They also find it difficult to build habits that promote sustainable living.

 

Who are our users?

  • Environmentally conscious people and/or
  • People wanting to save money

 

What we plan to address with our system:

  • A product that is attached to the meter box of a household, that connects to an app.
  • An application platform to view and visualise energy consumption on a daily up to a yearly basis, compared to the ideal consumption levels set by the government, as well as to
  • Users can gain insights from future consumption prediction models and receive suggestions for lowering their levels to both conserve energy as well as lower the cost per month.
  • Users can log/track their progress in terms of implementing sustainable practices, they can maintain a streak and based on user data and habits, the app would also deliver targeted notifications with actionable tips for further energy reduction/info on the importance of reducing carbon footprint.

WIREFRAMES

"EcoSync" is an app I designed to help users track their carbon footprint by analyzing their daily activities, like energy consumption and travel habits. The goal was to create an intuitive and visually appealing tool that empowers users to make more sustainable choices.

 

The app goes beyond just tracking; it offers personalized insights and recommendations tailored to each user’s lifestyle. Whether it’s reducing energy usage or cutting down on waste, "EcoSync" provides actionable steps to help users transition toward more eco-friendly habits. A key feature of the app is the ability for users to set sustainability goals and monitor their progress over time, encouraging long-term commitment to reducing their carbon footprint.

 

To match the app's eco-conscious mission, I chose a color scheme of muted greens and yellows, which fosters a calm and environmentally friendly vibe. The soft tones reinforce the app’s focus on sustainability, creating a soothing yet impactful user experience that reflects the positive environmental changes it aims to inspire.
