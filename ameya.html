Website designed for a biotechnology startup

I had the chance to design a website blueprint for "Ameya Engineering Solutions," a biotechnology startup focused on innovative products in the biotech space. The challenge was to create a site that effectively showcased their single product while maintaining a professional and polished feel. The company’s branding was central to the design, with a blue and orange color palette drawn from their logo.

 

The design approach was to keep things simple yet impactful. With only one product to highlight, I aimed for a clean layout that emphasized clarity and ease of navigation. The blue tones conveyed trust and reliability, while the orange added a pop of energy and innovation, balancing the professional and creative aspects of the brand.

 

By focusing on a streamlined user experience, the site allows potential customers to easily learn about the product’s benefits and technological advancements. The result was a design that not only reflected the brand’s forward-thinking approach to biotechnology but also created an engaging platform for showcasing their work.
