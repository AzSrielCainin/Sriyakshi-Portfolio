Product Description

"BYANA" is an app I designed to simplify the process of booking BMTC bus tickets. With busy commuters in mind, the app offers a quick search feature to find the nearest bus stops and optimal routes. It also provides real-time bus tracking, ensuring users can see arrival times and even get updates on how crowded the bus is before boarding.

 

To make the travel experience even smoother, "BYANA" includes notifications for when a bus is arriving, as well as reminders when it’s time to get off at your stop. One of the standout features is seat reservation, which allows users to secure a comfortable spot ahead of time. I also added parental controls, giving parents the ability to book and track their children’s journeys, providing peace of mind.

 

The app’s vibrant blue color scheme reflects both energy and reliability, making it visually engaging while conveying a sense of trust and ease. The design is clean and efficient, ensuring a hassle-free experience for everyday commuters and families alike, helping them navigate the city with confidence.
