Redesign of Swayam- A government owned E-Learning Platform
Product Description

After conducting an intensive usability test, I embarked on redesigning the "Swayam" website to address the shortcomings identified during the evaluation. The primary goal was to enhance the overall user experience, ensuring that users could navigate the site effortlessly and find the information they needed without frustration.

 

Accessibility was a key consideration in the redesign, allowing the website to cater to a wider audience, including those with disabilities. By incorporating best practices for accessibility, I aimed to create a welcoming environment for every user, ensuring that everyone could engage with the content fully.

 

To align the site with contemporary web standards and trends, I modernized the design while retaining a familiar brand identity. By blending modern design elements with user-centric features, the redesigned "Swayam" website stands as a testament to a thoughtful approach to user experience, accessibility, and contemporary aesthetics.
