Website designed for an clothing e-commerce company

I had the opportunity to work on a project for "4 Four Squares," an established clothing brand based in India. My task was to create a blueprint for their website, focusing on delivering an experience that matched the brand’s identity. It was all about sophistication and subtlety, with a sleek design that captured the essence of modern elegance.

 

The website’s design leaned into muted tones—soft greys and blacks—giving it a clean, minimalist look. I used transparent buttons to enhance the elegant feel, allowing the design to breathe and maintain a light, airy vibe. The absence of bright colors made it stand out as a timeless piece, with every element working together to draw the user into a serene browsing experience.

 

One of the key inspirations was the feeling of autumn, which I translated into the overall aesthetic. It’s a website that evokes calm and reflection, much like a crisp autumn day. The subtlety in design choices ensures that the brand’s fashion offerings take center stage, creating a smooth and engaging user journey that is both beautiful and functional.
